Key Takeaways for Speedverse

Creator-Led Acquisition Dominates

Stake built $2.6B+ revenue primarily through streamer and influencer partnerships (Drake, Trainwreckstv, xQc). Allocate 35%+ of marketing budget to creator partnerships. Speedverse has a unique advantage: using the product IS creating marketing content.

Predictions Are More Shareable Than Bets

Polymarket proved that sharing predictions is inherently social and less stigmatized than gambling. "I think this video will go viral" is an opinion worth sharing. This gives Speedverse a higher viral coefficient (K) than traditional gambling apps.

Simplification Wins Market Share

Prize Picks surpassed DraftKings in DFS entries by simplifying the experience to just over/under picks. Sleeper won dynasty fantasy by making it social. Speedverse should strip predictions to their simplest form: watch, predict, win.

Token Economics Create Flywheels

Rollbit's RLB token created a speculative flywheel: token price rises attract attention, bringing users, generating revenue, funding buybacks. A SPEED token can create aligned incentives across users, creators, and the platform.
